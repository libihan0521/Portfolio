Bihan Li

Product Designer
Brand Designer

Hello, I’m Bihan. In Chinese, “Bi” (碧) represents the clarity of jade, while “Han” (涵) conveys the inclusiveness of deep, calm water. The hope my parents embedded in my name have also shaped my design philosophy.

Currently, I serve as the Design Lead at an e-mobility company, where I translate this philosophy into practice. Leading the rebranding and digital transformation of a fast-growing business, I’ve learned how clarity can align complex systems and how inclusiveness can bridge diverse teams, customers, and cultures.

At work, my superpower is breaking down complex problems into clear, actionable steps, making it easier for teams to understand and execute. I believe in Descartes’ methodology—that any complex issue can be decomposed into simpler parts and systematically resolved. I’m also skilled at building workflows to boost efficiency.

In my spare time, I enjoy watching films and TV series, as well as drawing comics. Making people laugh with my comics gives me a strong sense of accomplishment. My designer’s eye also enriches how I experience entertainment—for example, noticing how the color system in Breaking Bad supports its storytelling and character development, or how comic storytelling techniques can be applied to enhance the immersion and delight of design.
