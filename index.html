What to know more about me?

My name is Bihan Li (李碧涵). "bi-碧" represents pure green, and "han-涵" signifies ocean-deep water.

As a designer, I enjoy learning from different disciplines and embracing diverse perspectives. I felt design adds depth to my existence by providing a unique lens that magnifies and colorizes the world.

I believe design goes beyond decoration and involves a strong business perspective, humanistic care, and effective problem-solving. It fosters connections between users and products, creates engaging experiences, and drives positive outcomes.

In my spare time, I love exploring creative technologies and creating manga art.
