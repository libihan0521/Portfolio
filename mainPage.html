ABOUT

I am Bihan Li (李碧涵). "bi-碧" means pure green, and "han-涵" means ocean-vast deep water.

 

I learned Landscape Architecture in college. But then I found I am more interested in designing things combined with business, media, and technology instead of terrain, architecture, and plants. Therefore, I pursue an MFA degree in the Design and Technology program at Parsons.

I stepped into various design disciplines during my graduate years. Then I found I like the UX design the most. Because UX designers are always encouraged to be generalists who embrace knowledge from various disciplines, designing user experience can lead me to look through others’ views and understand different cultures. I simply enjoy the bigger world refracted by the UX lens. So I want to take UX design as my career choice.

 

In my spare time, you can find me drawing manga, I think manga can also be considered a branch of the design discipline, characters, and plots are the subjects to be designed.
