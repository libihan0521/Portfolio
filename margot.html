Margot's Bustling Brain

TIME

2022.11~2022.12

TYPE

The final project of the Sound Design course / Sole work

INSTRUCTOR

Josh Barrell

TOOL

ProTools, Procreate

The short animation tells the story of Margot, a girl who is constantly concerned about her safety, causing various dramas in her mind. I named her after the female character who is attacked by a murderer behind the green curtain in Hitchcock's film Dial M for Murder. My inspiration for this character is my observation that there are some experiences of fear that almost exclusively belong to females.
