Background knowledge

The final design of this project is a game-based product centered around emotion journaling. If you're not familiar with those products, I've prepared a slide for you to explore the background knowledge. This will provide you with a better understanding of the value that "Bunny Mood" brings. However, if you have already grasped the value of "Bunny" from the limited information above, feel free to skip this section.

In the current market, there exists a diverse range of emotion journal tools that can be classified into three types: physical products, digital applications, and the indirect type involving some social interactions.

Physical Products

  • Card games or notepads specifically designed to help children recognize and express their emotions.
  • Notebooks featuring specially designed templates for recording daily emotions.

Digital Applications

There is a wide range of mobile apps available that enhance the experience of mood-tracking. These apps provide interactive features, enjoyable customization tools, and helpful alerts. In addition to the essential functions of mood logging and visualizing emotion data, these applications offer unique features that enrich user experience. Some apps even incorporate clinical intervention techniques such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), attention-shifting strategies, forward-looking strategies, positive self-talk (reframing), and more.

The indirect type involving some social interactions

  • Engage with a supportive therapist who encourages you to express your emotions during each session.
  • Confide in a close friend, partner, or parent who creates a safe space for you to freely share your feelings.
  • Connect and share your emotions with others through social media platforms.

Interview notes

To gain valuable insights and generate ideas, I conducted semi-structured interviews with 19 individuals. Recognizing the benefits of increasing emotional awareness for everyone, I intentionally sought interviewees from diverse age groups and backgrounds, without narrowing down the target users at this stage. By primarily interviewing my acquaintances rather than strangers, I aimed to establish a personal connection between their personalities and their answers, enabling a more comprehensive analysis of the outcomes. (The interview outline)

Reviews on Apple Store

Utilizing reviews on the Apple Store provides an additional avenue for user research. In order to gather insights into users' perceptions regarding specific features and experiences, I conducted an analysis of their attitudes using a card-sorting panel based on these collected reviews.

Feedback delay is the top reason why users quit an emotion journal product.

The primary positive feedback received in emotion tracking is when users revisit their old journals, triggering nostalgic memories or discovering patterns within their emotions.

However, the time required to accumulate a sufficient number of journals to attain such positive feedback is often lengthy. Additionally, the process of journaling itself can be draining for many individuals. If they miss a week of recording, it often leads to a longer gap of a month, eventually resulting in abandonment. Thus, establishing the habit of consistent journaling becomes a significant challenge. The primary reason users discontinue using an emotion journal product is due to the delay in receiving feedback.

Factors that contribute to users' continued engagement:

  • Elegant and visually appealing design: The interface is designed with a stylish and attractive aesthetic, captivating users with its visual appeal.
  • User-friendly logging features: The logging features are designed to provide a comfortable and seamless experience, enabling users to record rich and meaningful content concisely and effortlessly.
  • Comprehensive statistics and analysis: The inclusion of in-depth statistics empowers users to gain insights and analyze patterns within their emotional data, facilitating a deeper understanding of their emotional well-being.
  • Engaging and supportive communities: The presence of active and supportive communities fosters a sense of belonging and encourages users to connect, share experiences, and receive valuable support from like-minded individuals.

Persona

Reaching the final design of "Bunny Mood" wasn't an immediate process. Honestly, I even didn't reach a clear design goal like "creating a game for logging emotions" initially. Instead, I set out with the obscure design goal of "designing something for people to better understand their emotions".
