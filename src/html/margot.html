Margot's Bustling Brain

INSTRUCTOR: Josh Barrell       TIME: 2022.11~2022.12
TYPE: The final project of the Sound Design course / Sole work
Tool: ProTools, Procreate


The short animation tells the story of Margot, a girl who is constantly concerned about her safety, causing various dramas in her mind. I named her after the female character who is attacked by a murderer behind the green curtain in Hitchcock's film Dial M for Murder.
 
My inspiration for this character is my observation that there are some experiences of fear that almost exclusively belong to females. For example, when taking a taxi, my male friends always naturally start casual and pleasant conversations with taxi drivers, while my female friends would be nervous, and seldom interact with the drivers.
