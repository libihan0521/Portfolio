Roblox & Parsons

In the 2023 spring semester, I attended the collaborative course between Parsons School of Design and Roblox. In this course, we utilized Roblox's innovative layered clothing tool to create a collection. I contributed two looks to the final collection: the golden feather and the water dress. The golden feather design was my sole creation, and I was in charge of the 3D modeling and weight painting for the water dress.
